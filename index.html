Provider and Clinical Laboratory Link - Connections between Hospital and Nursing Home Providers with Associated Labs

April 25, 2023

Why Connect Providers with their Labs?

Within a given area, the number of labs greatly out numbers the number of providers. The relative location of labs to their affiliate provider also varies. While some labs are located within the same building location as their provider, some labs exist at their own building facility. In many cases, the connection or awareness of a provider + lab affiliation is unknown. Through our project, we have established a clear connection between a provider and their associated labs based on physical location via geocoding and address matching. Moreover, we can indicate to providers the available labs near a hospital or nursing home.

Our Goal:

Through the linkage of providers (i.e. hospitals and nursing homes) with their affiliated labs, we strive to make an efficient system for patients to find their facilities. In doing so, we also allow providers and lab entities to verify the correct linkage of associations.

How Does this Work?

With the direct connection between providers and labs, we allow for an efficient relay of information to patients and other providers. We have created an interactive dashboard that allows users to search for associated facilities based on the limited information they have. Using search queries through facility names, location address, or provider information, we provide a vehicle for customers to find connected labs or further provider information.
